Artifact: prototypes/creator-mvp-loop-v0-1.html

This prototype shows the creator-facing product workflow rather than raw internal artifacts. The creator can inspect the inferred direction, see status, review evidence, and route satisfaction feedback.

  • Idea intake remains natural language and editable.
  • HumanGate is a normal decision state for scope, risk, or authority.
  • Stop visibly disables automatic continuation.
  • Continue routes to HumanGate when it would expand the authorized slice.
Known limitation

Static sample data only. It does not connect to real Loop state, verifier output, research, git, or Knowledge Base storage.

Evidence Summary

Checks Static artifact exists, required controls visible, state model represented, no dependency surface.

Known risk Semantic fit still requires human verification against the demand contract.
Decision need Feedback is needed before Done, Repair, HumanGate, or Stop.

Satisfaction Feedback

Every feedback option maps to a concrete route; scope expansion goes to HumanGate and does not disappear into chat memory.

Correction Routing

Feedback pending

Choose a feedback control to see whether the system routes to Done, bounded Repair, revised Demand Contract, HumanGate, or Stop.

  • Adjust: bounded repair inside authorized prototype scope.
  • Reject: reopen direction or Demand Contract instead of patching blindly.
  • Continue: HumanGate when the next slice expands scope.
  • Stop: automatic continuation is halted.
